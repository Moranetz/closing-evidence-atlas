**Status legend:**
- `gold` — published in fully OA journal under a permissive license.
- `green` — published toll-access but author-version freely available in a repository.
- `hybrid` — published toll-access in subscription journal but article-level OA license paid.
- `bronze` — freely readable on publisher site but no OA license.
- `closed` — paywalled, no OA version known.
- `not_in_unpaywall` — DOI not indexed by Unpaywall.
- `no_doi` — record has no DOI in the corpus (e.g., ClinicalTrials.gov records, some preprints).
